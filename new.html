Globally Accessible Opportunities (GOA)

GOA is a market intelligence system that harnesses market demand for goods, services, ideas, and technologies, and converts them into end-to-end entrepreneurial opportunities for investment by graduates.

Key GOA Activities:

  • Global Market Alertness (GMA) - Monitoring international markets for demand opportunities.
  • Thorough business planning - Covering everything from agriculture, storage, logistics, and export strategies.
  • Triple-E compliance - Ensuring efficiency, effectiveness, and excellence in all business processes.
  • Adequate financing - Mobilizing resources to ensure smooth business operations.
  • World-class supply chain & logistics - Eliminating inefficiencies and optimizing international trade processes.
  • Excellence in export reputation - Establishing credibility for Kenyan exports through strict compliance.
  • One-stop-shop export clearance warehouses - Simplifying export logistics for all stakeholders.
  • Host nation bonded depots - Facilitating smooth entry and distribution of Kenyan goods abroad.
  • Export reporting through Promasters Postgraduate Performance Scorecard - Tracking success and areas for improvement.

Promasters Postgraduate Support System (PPSS) Rollout

The PPSS is a structured initiative aimed at supporting graduates in transitioning into productive entrepreneurial careers. This system focuses on maximizing entrepreneurial productivity through well-defined strategies.

Key Elements of PPSS:

  • Globally Accessible Opportunities (GOA): Shifting focus from production to market to empower entrepreneurs.
  • Proper Institutions, Structures & Systems: Establishing the National Postgraduate Empowerment Center (NPEC) and other support networks.
  • One-Pager Reporting & Accountability: Daily report discipline for businesses and postgraduate centers.
  • Seed Capital & Financial Discipline: Supporting graduates with capital while ensuring business discipline.

Phase-Based Implementation

The rollout of PPSS will occur in structured phases:

  • Phase 1: Establishing solid institutions to support 10 million Kenyan graduates.
  • Phase 2: Providing structured postgraduate support before graduation to ensure smooth transition.
  • Phase 3: Mainstreaming graduates from previous years who lacked structured entrepreneurial support.

PPSS aims to create self-sufficient entrepreneurs who contribute to innovation, economic growth, and job creation.
